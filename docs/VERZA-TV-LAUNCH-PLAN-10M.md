This plan outlines a $10.35M launch strategy across 12 months designed to acquire 500,000+ subscribers, generate $15M+ ARR, and establish Verza TV as the definitive American brand in vertical entertainment.

The strategy leverages Alan Mruvka's E! Entertainment pedigree, an aggressive paid acquisition machine, culturally targeted content marketing, and a creator-driven organic flywheel that compounds over time.

### 2.2 Creative Strategy

**Ad Format Priority:**
1. **Hook Clips (3–8 seconds)** — The most dramatic moment from episode 1, cut mid-sentence. "She found out her husband's secret... watch free on Verza TV." These drive 70%+ of installs on TikTok and Reels.
2. **Before/After Story Arcs (15 seconds)** — Show episode 1 vs. episode 50 transformation. "She started as a maid. She ended as CEO."
3. **Reaction-Style UGC** — Creators watching a scene and reacting. "I can't believe she did that." Feels organic, outperforms polished ads 3:1.
4. **Cliffhanger Trailers (30 seconds)** — Mini movie trailers ending on the biggest cliffhanger. "New episodes every day. Free on Verza TV."
5. **Comparison Ads** — "If you loved [popular Netflix show], you'll binge this in one sitting."

**Creative Volume:** Produce 80–120 new ad creatives per month. Test 20 per week. Kill underperformers within 48 hours. Scale winners aggressively.

**Creative Team Required:**
- 2 performance creative editors (full-time)
- 1 motion graphics designer
- 1 creative strategist / scriptwriter
- UGC creator network (50+ on retainer)
